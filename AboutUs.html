About

This is a website dedicated to the Metal community made by one person. The goal of this website is to grow the Metal community and meet new people but also discuss certain topics!

Rules

1. Topic

1.1 Please do not post stuff that is not related in any way to any subgenre of Rock music. You can post anything as long as it is related to this main topic (including song covers, outfits and collections)
1.2 Do not post stuff that is 18+ (such as gore, porn, dangerous activities, etc.)
1.3 Do not post any illegal activities. This site will not be held accountable for your actions!

2. Registration & login

2.1 Do not make a ton of accounts. It is understandable that you make a couple if you lost one or want to stay anonymous, but please do not make a ton of accounts (user counting will be more accurate)
2.2 Do not log in into someone else`s account without thier consent!

3. Copyright

3.1 Do not make copies of this website for your own gain (or for any reason!)
3.2 You can use the images/gifs used for this site as long as you give credit to the creators

4. Promotion

4.1 Do not use this site to promote companies, videogames, servers, discord servers, groups, other apps, merchendise, tickets or other websites
4.2 If you want to use this site to promote something (legally), you need the admin`s consent, contact on the matter here
